Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026
Markhor is a pre-launch company. Today this website does one thing with personal data: it collects an email address if you choose to join the early-access list. The platform and its regulated KYC processes are not yet live.

Who we are

This website is operated by Markhor (“we”, “us”), a company being established in Pakistan as a Myco Holdings venture. For any privacy question, contact us via the form on our home page.

What we collect

  • Email address — only if you submit it through the early-access form. We use it solely to contact you about Markhor’s launch and early access.
  • Basic technical data — standard server logs from our hosting provider (e.g. IP address, browser type) used for security and to keep the site running.

We do not collect financial, identity (KYC), or payment information on this site. Those processes will only exist on the regulated platform once it launches, and will be governed by a separate, dedicated privacy notice and the applicable PVARA, SECP and destination-country rules.

How we use it

To send you launch and early-access updates, and to respond to your enquiries. We do not sell your data, and we don’t share it except with the service providers that help us run this site (our hosting and form provider).

Third-party services

This site uses a hosting and forms provider to deliver pages and capture early-access submissions. Fonts are self-hosted, so no third-party font service is used. Some pages embed videos hosted on YouTube, which load only when you tap play; at that point YouTube (in privacy-enhanced “no-cookie” mode) may receive your request and limited technical data. We use no advertising or tracking cookies.

Your choices

You can ask us to remove your email from the early-access list at any time — just contact us and we’ll delete it. When data-protection rules apply to you, you may have rights to access, correct or erase your information.

Changes

We’ll update this notice as Markhor moves from concept to a regulated, operating platform. The “last updated” date above always reflects the current version.
