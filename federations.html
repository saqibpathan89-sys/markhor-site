The offer

Everything a federation needs, on one rail.

Not a platform you have to learn. A set of regulated tools your federation switches on, keeps control of, and reports through.

Supporter tokens

Issue a capped, disclosed supporter token tied to a published slate. Fans and diaspora back it; proceeds are ring-fenced and released against milestones.

Transparency ledger

A live, public dashboard of revenue and allocation. The credibility a federation usually spends years and PR budgets trying to build, on by default.

Programmable ticketing

Verifiable provenance, resale and anti-scalping rules in the contract, and settlement straight to the federation in real time.

Diaspora reach

One verified wallet puts your federation in front of 11 million Pakistanis abroad, compliant in the UK, UAE, US and Canada.

Athlete pipeline

Let young squad members raise regulated career-shares to fund training, with backers protected by escrow and vesting.

Compliance, handled

SECP investor-protection rules, KYC/AML, issuance caps and audit logs are built into the rail. You focus on sport, not paperwork.
