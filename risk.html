Risk Disclosure

Last updated: June 2026
Markhor’s instruments are not yet available. The figures, instruments and charts shown on this site are illustrative examples — not real offers, prices, forecasts or promised returns. This page explains the risks that will apply once regulated instruments go live.

Capital is at risk

Cultural and athlete-economy instruments — fan tokens, athlete career-shares, royalty NFTs and similar — carry risk. Their value can fall as well as rise, and you may get back less than you put in, or nothing. Never commit money you cannot afford to lose.

Illustrative figures are not forecasts

Any percentages, funding levels, holder counts, royalty rates and price charts shown on this website are examples used to explain how the platform will work. They are not predictions, guarantees, or indications of future performance or returns.

Regulated, but still developing

Markhor intends to operate within the joint PVARA–SECP regulatory sandbox. Sandbox supervision reduces certain risks through issuance caps, disclosure, escrow and investor-protection rules — but it does not eliminate risk, guarantee any outcome, or guarantee that any particular instrument will be approved, launched, or available.

Liquidity & time horizon

Some instruments may have lock-ups, vesting, cooling-off periods, or limited secondary markets. You may not be able to sell or redeem when you want, or at the price you expect.

Where you live matters

Availability and treatment of instruments depend on your jurisdiction. Diaspora users are subject to destination-country rules (for example UK FCA, UAE VARA, US FinCEN, Canada FINTRAC). Some products may not be available to you.

Not advice

Nothing on this site is financial, investment, legal or tax advice. Before participating in any future instrument, read its specific terms and disclosures and, if needed, seek independent professional advice.
